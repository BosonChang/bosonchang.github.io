This is Boson Chang — an adventurer, a thinker, a curious soul, and maybe a gamer. This website was created while he was sick at home with nothing else to do, so he thought a masked picture would suit the mood.


"I like to think about life, philosophy, and creative ideas all the time." -Chang, at the time of writing this.

He hated all readings and liberal arts when he was younger. Cursed by his "intelligence," he chained himself to the mindset that STEM development was the only contribution to the world. Now, it is believed that he has learned his lessons and has discovered the beauty in the world.


“我想吃麥當勞” -Chang, at the time when he was hungry

He might be a funny person (maybe not), depending on who you are. He's getting there. He is not good at talking to females (obviously because he is a STEM boy). He gets addicted to whatever is in front of him and stays addicted until reaching his goal.

As this picture suggests, he has a really strong adaptability. This ability helped him with traveling and going to a school with barely anything fun around. His insane addiction and self-control (only when needed) aid his success in many fields of study.


“睡哪裡都可以啊” -Chang, at the time of trips (any)

His hairstyle is like a lottery—I believe that’s the case for most people out there as well. Bad communication skills with the barber caused the instability and inconsistency in his hairstyle. It is never too terrible, though. Who took this picture, you might ask? It was one of my best friends. Not having a lot of friends, I value all of my friendships greatly. Although I barely reach out actively to most of them most of the time, I still love them from the bottom of my heart. The lack of socializing was not caused by anything—I just became better at enjoying solitude (a bit too much, though). Solitude provides me time to appreciate my surroundings and rethink the purpose of life. The purpose of life is definitely not what I say when playing video games.
